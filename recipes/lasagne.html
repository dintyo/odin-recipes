Lasagne

A delicious red wine lasagne

This lasagne is made with cheese and red wine, very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Cheese
  • Beef mince
  • Red Wine

Steps

  1. Layer a sheet of lasagne in a pan
  2. Layer some mince and cheese
  3. Layer another sheet of lasagne
  4. Pour red wine over the whole thing
  5. Put in the oven
  6. Cook until there are flames
  7. Serve to your enemies
